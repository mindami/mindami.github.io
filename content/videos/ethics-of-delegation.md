When an AI agent makes a consequential mistake—misreads a contract, places a bad trade, misroutes a patient—who is responsible? The developer? The deployer? The human who set the goal?

This panel brings together an ethicist, a legal scholar, and two practitioners to work through these questions with the nuance they deserve.
