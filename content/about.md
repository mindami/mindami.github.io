## About Mindami

*Mindami* is about being human in the agentic age.

We are exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, autonomous systems, and human experience with a focus on how these technologies will shape the way we work, create, and relate to each other.

### Core Tenets

Right now one of the most important questions in AI is **"How do humans maintain a meaningful relationship with the systems we build?"**

- Technology should serve people, not the other way around
- Autonomy and accountability must evolve together
- The people building these systems have a responsibility to think carefully about what they're creating
- Human oversight is critical
- Don't outsource your own thinking

### Who We Are

*Mindami* is an independent publication. We are not affiliated with any AI company, and we accept no advertising from AI vendors.
