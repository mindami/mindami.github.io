## About Agentic

*Agentic* is a publication about being human in the agentic age.

We cover the intersection of artificial intelligence, autonomous systems, and human experience—not from a place of hype or fear, but from genuine curiosity about how this technology will shape the way we work, create, and relate to each other.

### What We Believe

The most important question in AI right now isn't "what can agents do?" It's "what should they do, and how do we keep humans in a meaningful relationship with the systems we build?"

We believe:

- Technology should serve people, not the other way around
- Autonomy and accountability must evolve together
- The people building these systems have a responsibility to think carefully about what they're creating
- Complexity is not inevitable—clarity and craft still matter

### Who We Are

*Agentic* is an independent publication. We are not affiliated with any AI company, and we accept no advertising from AI vendors.
